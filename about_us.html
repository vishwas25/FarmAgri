Our Objective

To increase the profit margin of the farmers. To improve access to investment, technology, inputs and markets for farmers. To create linkages between the companies and farmers. To provide intouch connectivity for farmers and the agri products. To minimizing broker commision. To use information technology for crop production,digital payment.

Our Vision

To increase farmers’ income by transforming affiliated FPOs into value creation organizations and increase the profit margin This may also implies to increase productivity by adopting scientific practices and acknowledging right guidance ; save costs through collective bargaining for inputs and earn better returns through value addition and access to markets

Mission

To make member Farmer Producer Organizaitions (FPOs) economically viable, democratic, and self-governing To provide support to member FPOs through qualified and experienced resources To mobilize the required assistance and resources – policy action, inputs, technical knowledge, financial resources, and infrastructure To remove hurdles in enabling farmers’ access the markets directly or through member FPOs, both as buyers and seller
